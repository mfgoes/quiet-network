Haarlem Locations Guide

A curated list of notable places in Haarlem, Noord-Holland, Netherlands.

⭐ Main Attractions & Sights

The St. Bavo Church (Grote Kerk)
Historic church on the Grote Markt, famously played by Mozart.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Grote+Kerk+Haarlem

Frans Hals Museum
Dutch Golden Age art and works by Frans Hals.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Frans+Hals+Museum+Haarlem

Teylers Museum
The Netherlands’ oldest museum with art and science collections.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Teylers+Museum+Haarlem

Windmill De Adriaan (1779)
Traditional Dutch windmill on the Spaarne river.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Windmill+De+Adriaan+Haarlem

Ten Boom Museum
WWII history museum in the former home of Corrie ten Boom.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Ten+Boom+Museum+Haarlem

Amsterdamse Poort
Medieval city gate and remnant of Haarlem’s old walls.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Amsterdamse+Poort+Haarlem

Town Hall Haarlem (Stadhuis)
Historic city hall located on the Grote Markt.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Town+Hall+Haarlem

Archeological Museum Haarlem
Local archaeology collections near the market.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Archeological+Museum+Haarlem

Verwey Museum Haarlem
Local history museum.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Verwey+Museum+Haarlem

Teylers Hofje
Traditional Dutch hofje (almshouse courtyard garden).
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Teylers+Hofje+Haarlem

Proveniershuis
Historic hofje complex in Haarlem’s old town.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Proveniershuis+Haarlem

De Bolwerken
Scenic green belt along former bastions.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=De+Bolwerken+Haarlem

Haarlemmer Kweektuin
Garden and plant nursery with café.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Haarlemmer+Kweektuin

NZH Transport Museum
Transport history museum with vintage vehicles.
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=NZH+Transport+Museum+Haarlem
